About Us

Working Equitation Here at Majestic Equestrian our focus is on helping riders and horses to develop a closer partnership through this fairly new discipline. Despite it's infancy the sport of Working Equitation has proven extremely popular throughout the world. The sport involves four phasesâ€”working dressage, ease of handling with obstacles, speed with obstacles, and at the highest-level events, cattle handling. The sport tests the horse and rider's partnership and ability to manoeuvre obstacles. We offer lessons in all aspects of Working Equitation. If you would like to improve your riding skills and deepen your connection with your horse while learning a fun new sport then please contact us. We have our own trusted school masters if you don't have a horse of your own.

Trail Rides Majectic Equestrian also offers fantastic trail rides across our stunning working station on the edge of beautiful Lake Wanaka. We offer shorter trails starting from one hour to longer overnight mustering excursions so you can really get a feel for the life of the cowboy.
