About Us

Majestic Equestrian is lucky enough to be located in one of the most beautiful parts of New Zealand. Our property boarders the awe inspiring Lake Wanaka, we are not short of breath taking views in this part of the world. We offer fantastic facilities and trustworthy school masters. If you want to ride or train in a safe friendly enviroment with the added bonus of amazing scenery you've come to the right place.

Working Equitation Here at Majestic Equestrian our focus is on helping riders and horses to develop a closer partnership through this fairly new discipline. Despite it's infancy the sport of Working Equitation has proven extremely popular throughoutthe world. The sport involves four key skills; working dressage, ease of handling with obstacles, speed with obstacles, and at the highest-level events, cattle handling. The sport tests the horse and rider's partnership and ability to manoeuvre obstacles. We offer lessons in all aspects of Working Equitation. If you would like to improve your riding skills and deepen your connection with your horse while learning a fun new sport then please contact us.

Trail Rides Majectic Equestrian also offers fantastic trail rides across our stunning working station on the edge of beautifulLake Wanaka. We offer scenic trail rides as well as wine tasting trail rides and for the really adventurous longer overnight camping excursions so you can really get a feel for the life of the cowboy. All trail rides are a fantastic way to experience the beauty of Wanaka and the surrounding areas.
